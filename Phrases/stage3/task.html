The whole point of the app is to send you push notifications. In this stage, you will achieve that goal!

Objectives

  1. Create a notification channel for your app.
  2. Remember that TextView with "No reminders set" we've created in the first stage? Set an OnClickListener which shows a TimePicker dialog, which we'll use for setting notification time.
  3. Create a notification with a title "Your phrase of the day" and a random text from your phrases (that's exactly why you need at least three phrases)
  4. Update the TextView to "Reminder set at $hour:$minute" and post a notification at that exact time. AlarmManager will come in handy there.
